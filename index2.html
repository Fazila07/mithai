Our Story
"I didn't want to give up mithai. I wanted to make it better."

Growing up, sweets were how we celebrated everything — from Diwali to just surviving a tough week. But as I started paying attention to what went into them — maida, vanaspati, artificial colours, compound chocolate — something didn't sit right.

Mithai 2.0 started in my kitchen with one question: can traditional Indian sweets be made with honest ingredients, without compromising on taste? Two years and hundreds of recipe experiments later — yes. Absolutely yes.

Every product we make is something I'd happily give my own family. That's the only standard we know.

Priya Sharma Founder, Mithai 2.0 · Home Cook turned Honest Baker
